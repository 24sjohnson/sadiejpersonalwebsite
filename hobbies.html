My Hobbies & Interests

What I Love to Do

Explore my various interests and activities that keep me inspired and energized. These hobbies are important to me because they are activities that I can enjoy with my friends and family.

Theater & Musical Productions

Hamilton

My introduction to musical theater and still my favorite production. Hamilton perfectly combines American History with modern musical elements. The cast's performance was absolutely incredible, making history come alive in an engaging way.

  • Performed at the Orpheum Theatre
  • First musical experience
  • Favorite musical production

Six on Broadway

During my senior year of high school, I had the incredible opportunity to experience Six on Broadway in New York. The energy and atmosphere of a Broadway show was absolutely unforgettable, making me eager to return for more productions.

  • Broadway experience in New York
  • Senior year highlight
  • Unique modern take on history

Grease at Chanhassen

Recently enjoyed Grease at the Chanhassen Dinner Theater, combining great entertainment with dining. The dinner theater format added an extra layer of enjoyment to the classic musical, creating a truly memorable evening.

  • Dinner theater experience
  • Classic musical production
  • Interactive atmosphere
