Explore my journey at the University of Minnesota Duluth and discover what makes this institution special.

Duluth's Features

What I like about Duluth:

  • I enjoy a nice breeze and for it to be cooler, and Duluth is just that.
  • Duluth is very close to state parks such as Jay Cooke, Gooseberry, and Moose Lake.
  • Bellisio's Italian restaurant is located right in Canal Park of Duluth and has amazing food.

Why UMD:

I knew I wanted to go to a college in the University of Minnesota system and was between the U in the Twin Cities and UMD.

    Factors:

  • Was 2 hours away from home, but not too far away that I wouldn't be able to visit my friends and family often.
  • Duluth having the beautiful Lake Superior and lots of greenery was another reason I chose UMD.
  • The size of the campus was a big factor for me. I wanted a walkable campus and to be able to find classes easily. UMD is big enough that it isn't like high school, but not as big as the Twin Cities Campus where you could get lost easily.
